SIGN UP TO ATTEND COSSIEPLAY EVENTS

Join in on our monthly Cosplay events!

On the first Saturday of every month we hold a “CossiePlay Party” at the town hall. Entry is $15 for adults, $10 for ages 6-17 and kids under 6 are free. Each entry includes one non-alcoholic drink and a glow stick. Parties start at 6pm and run until 11pm. There is always a live DJ at the party (who takes requests) and dancing is common (but not required).

Monthly Deals & Discounts

  • First time partygoers get a 50% discount on entry
  • Purchase a custom costume & get free entry to the next CossiePlay event"
  • 3 for 2 drinks at the bar
  • Wear your costume to KFC on Stanton St. & get a free drink with the purchase of a 21-piece bucket (Remember to mention CossiePlay!)

 

Party Guidelines

  • Cosplayers attend at their own risk and are responsible for their own safety. Check with your doctor before you party hard!
  • Party Fees must be paid at the door (Cash or EFTPOS accepted, credit card payments incur a 2% surcharge.)
  • You must be 18 or over to consume alcohol on the premises.(Intoxicated persons will be escorted to a taxi paid for by partygoer)

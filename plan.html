Goals

The CossiePlay website is intended to promote and increase the attendance of CossiePlay’s monthly cosplay events, and sales of custom-made costumes by encouraging users to signup for Cossieplay events on the website, and promoting CossiePlay’s costume range on the homepage.   

Success Evaluation

  • Attendance numbers increased due to email signups from website
  • More custom-made costumes were sold due to increased attendance numbers at events, and costumes being displayed on the homepage

Target Audience

Cossieplay aims to target High School students to young adults, as a younger audience wanst to see the information quickly,therefore the site is designed to display CossiePlay's costumes and monthly events on the homepage informing new users immediately of CossiePlay's services as they enter the website. The CossiePlay signup button also displays on each page promoting the user to register for their monthly events.
